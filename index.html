About the Service

is-truly-a.pro is a service that allows anyone to get free and easy-to-remember subdomains. With a simple registration process, you can claim your own unique subdomain under the .is-truly-a.pro domain.

How to Register

To register your own subdomain, visit the is-truly-a.pro registration repo and follow the instructions.

Report Abuse

If you believe a subdomain is being used inappropriately, please report the issue so we can investigate and take appropriate action.
